What is FamSketch?

FamSketch is a word composed of two English expressions:
Family and Sketch.

This reflects the main goal of our project, which is in short the creation of a web application, portal and mobile applications based on a social network, for recording family information and sharing it between a narrow and precisely specified group of people - family members.

What can FamSketch do?

FamSketch will provide:

  • store and share photos, audio and video recordings that you want to show to your loved ones (recordings from weddings, christenings, ribbon, etc.)
  • writing statuses, chat, the ability to comment on posts and other standard social networking features.
  • creating a family tree, sorting and navigating between added family connections in a simple and graphically clear form.
  • the possibility of connecting not only with the family, but also with family acquaintances
  • recording important events, thoughts and observations of a family character, ie creating an online family chronicle, a parent's legacy, capturing your life story ...
  • the opportunity to print your profile and use it as a gift in an elegant printed form, the opportunity to inherit your digital life ...
  • other features and design customizations that are added during development and that we have ready for the next phases of the project.

What makes FamSketch different? (... like other social networks?)

Is FamSketch a service that is really missing from the market? Does it offer features that other portals such as Facebook, Google or Tumblr do not yet offer? How is it different from these services?

1. Specialization.

FamSketch is a specialized social network, which distinguishes it from the above-mentioned portals. Because Facebook, for example, does not have a clearly defined circle of people for whom it is intended, it currently causes a certain amount of information chaos when using it. The stories of your family members are mixed here with the stories of your work colleagues, former classmates, neighbors, etc

One of the sources of inspiration that led us to the idea of creating FamSketch is the LinkedIn portal. This service is a beautiful example of how to use the potential of a social network to help you in your career life. However, it should be noted that on LinkedIn, the user behaves differently on LinkedIn than on other social networks - by sharing mainly work experience and not photos of his lunch or favorite music.

Our goal is to achieve something similar, but with focus on family life. The user should feel the same comfort on our website as being surrounded in a family circle.

2. Simplicity.

Our service is intended for the whole family, ie for all generations. Therefore, using our application will be as simple and intuitive as possible, so that the older generation and people who are not familiar with Internet can still find it simple to use.

After entering the basic personal data, our system will automatically suggest connections to all your relatives who will already have a FamSketch account, so you won't need to spend any time looking them up.

All your connections will be clearly displayed in the form of a the form of a Family Tree.

3. Security.

At FamSketch, users will share their family experiences and information that is confidential, so one of the most important things we stand for is the security of our website and the guarantee of our users' privacy.

With us, you will be 100% sure that only your loved ones will see your shared posts, without complicated privacy settings.

We will only interfere with the privacy of our users to the minimum extent necessary, including the deletion of explicit, obscene or otherwise inappropriate posts and possible interference with activities that would be of an illegal nature.

We commit to never to trade with the information entrusted to us by our users, as well we will not pass on any information to a third party to users, just as we will not pass them on to any third party (as other social networks normally do.)
